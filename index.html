This year you've gotten your very own Kitty-Bejeweled!

Directions!

  1. Click on the tile you wish to pivot!
  2. Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to pivot the tile around the board
  3. Try to get at least five kitties/stars in a row or column
  4. When you do, each tile in the streak will be added to your scoreboard on the right. Then each of those tiles will be replaced with new ones
